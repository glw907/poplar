## Context

Subtle enough to recede behind key hints, clear
enough to read groups. Spacing alone was insufficient.

## Decision

`┊` (U+250A, light quadruple dash vertical) in
`fg_dim` between key groups. Custom rendering, not bubbles/help.

## Consequences

No follow-on notes recorded.
