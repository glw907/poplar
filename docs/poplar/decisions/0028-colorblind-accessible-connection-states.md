## Context

Triple redundancy ensures accessibility across
colorblind conditions, monochrome terminals, and screen readers.

## Decision

Connection states use shape + color + text: `●`
green filled (connected), `○` red hollow (offline), `◐` orange
half (reconnecting).

No follow-on notes recorded.
